Who We Are

NextMyles.com is an online facilitator connecting transporters, truck drivers, consumers and other related entities on one platform to ensure easy and profitable transportation. We provide an integrated platform to registered users to effectively move their cargo at the best prices. Registered transporters have easy access and availability of loads from the city they are in. We have a fully operational call center to support, verify and forward the requirements. All registrations are fully verified by our call center personnel. We are an integrated support system with risk mitigation tools, flexibility, secure payment gateway and excellent customer service. NextMyles enables the better utilization of the vehicle capacity and better vehicle availability to the customers..
